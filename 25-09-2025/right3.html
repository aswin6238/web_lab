Department of Computer Application

The Department of Computer Applications at MES College of Engineering (MESCE) in Kuttippuram offers a two-year professional Master of Computer Applications (MCA) degree, focusing on industry-relevant software engineering, practical skills, and the latest technologies. The department emphasizes both theoretical knowledge and hands-on experience, equipping students with the skills to become software engineers in the current IT landscape. It boasts eminent faculty, state-of-the-art lab facilities, and a well-stocked library to support student learning.
